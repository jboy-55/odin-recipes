Upgraded Instant Ramen

The perfect bowl of noodles for those that love ramen but are way too lazy to make it from scratch!

One of my favorite dishes in the world is Ramen. So many possibilities and combinations of flavor. It is impossible to get bored of it. But it can be a bit of a chore to make the perfect bowl from scratch.

This is my way of cheating the system and making a lazy, but still delicious, bowl of Ramen. This version is going to be spicy and flavorful with all of my favorite bits of this highly popular dish, but cooked in only a fraction of the time.

Ingredients

  • Your favorite pack of instant noodles
  • Flavor Packet that comes with the noodles
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 clove of Garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp of grated Ginger
  • 1/2 tbsp Toasted Sesame Oil
  • 1 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • Scallions
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Chili oil

Optional

  • Oyster Sauce
  • Hoisin Sauce
  • Fish Sauce
  • Tofu
  • Your Choice of Protein (Beef, Chicken, Shrimp, etc.)
  • Assorted Vegetables (Carrots, Mushrooms, Cabbage, Spinach, etc.)
  • Nori
  • Thai Basil

Steps

  1. Put a small sauce pot on medium heat and add the Sesame oil. Once hot add the minced garlic and grated ginger. Let it saute for 30 seconds to a minute (DO NOT LET IT BURN!). If you have any other veggies you want to saute, now is the time to do it.
  2. Once the garlic and ginger is sauteed add the soy sauce, Chili oil, and any other sauce you feel like adding. This will create the base of the broth.
  3. Now that everyone is getting along add 2 cups of water and bring to a boil.
  4. With the water now boiling, add the ramen noodles and let them cook for 3 minutes or so until they are tender.
  5. Add the flavor packet that came with the Ramen if you wish. If you have your own seasoning mix then add that instead.
  6. Bring the heat down to low and add the egg. DO NOT STIR! Let it sit for 6-7 minutes until the egg whites are cooked but the yolk is still nice and runny.
  7. Pour the ramen into your bowl and proceed to garnish it with all your desired fixings. In my case I am adding sesame seeds, chopped scallions, and more chili oil. I like it Spicy!!!
  8. ENJOY!!!
